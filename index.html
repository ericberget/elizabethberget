Inside the Book

What if motherhood wasn't a barrier
to knowing God — but an invitation?

Love Like a Mother weaves together real-life stories from the trenches of motherhood with overlooked maternal imagery for God in the Bible, offering both spiritual depth and a sense of humor that only a mother could deliver.

01

Rediscover God as Mother

An invitation to rediscover God under the lens of motherhood — as the one who gestates, births, mourns, nourishes, comforts, and protects. Mothers bear the image of God in the sacred ordinary, from first contractions to first tantrums.

02

A Lifeline for the Spiritually Adrift

For moms feeling spiritually adrift within parenting, a lifeline to connection right in the middle of dishes, diapers, and sleepless nights. Theological insights rooted in Scripture alongside contemplative practices that fit the busiest days.

03

For Every Stage of Motherhood

An inspiration for moms in all stages of life, perfect for gift-giving and group discussion. Whether you're in the thick of toddler years or watching your children leave home, these words will meet you exactly where you are.
