Love Like a Mother — Inside the Book

What if the exhaustion, wonder, and work of motherhood weren't barriers to knowing God…
but invitations?

Love Like a Mother weaves together real-life stories from the trenches of motherhood with overlooked maternal imagery for God in the Bible, offering both spiritual depth and a sense of humor that only a mother could deliver.

Rediscovering God as Mother

The book gently invites readers to rediscover God under the lens of motherhood, as the one who gestates, births, mourns, adopts, nourishes, comforts, and protects — just like a mother. This discovery highlights how mothers bear the image of God in the sacred ordinary of everyday motherhood, from first contractions to first smiles to first tantrums.

A Lifeline for the Spiritually Adrift

For moms feeling spiritually adrift within parenting, this book provides a lifeline to spiritual connection, right in the middle of dishes, diapers, and sleepless nights. It offers theological insights rooted in Scripture as well as contemplative practices that fit right within the busiest days. The book also serves as an inspiration to moms in all stages of life, perfect for gift-giving, Bible studies, book clubs, and more.
