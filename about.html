About Elizabeth

Hi! I'm Elizabeth.

I'm the author of Love Like a Mother: How the Sacred Work of Motherhood Reveals the Maternal Heart of God, releasing May 5, 2026 from Brazos Press. It's the book I desperately needed when my kids were little — a guide to the maternal imagery for God laced throughout Scripture, and an invitation to see how mothers bear the image of God right in the midst of diapers and dishes.

I'm married to Eric, who loves me so much that he lets me make to-do lists with him on Saturday mornings. Sometimes even before coffee. We live in Minneapolis with our three kids — Owen, Elsa, and Lewis — and one golden doodle named Penny who lives for stolen trash and chasing squirrels.

Some of my other party tricks: I'm a former public school ELL teacher, and I've lived in South Korea and South Sudan. For my day job, I homeschool my kids. I'm a textbook Enneagram 1, and I think the world of proper grammar. I love food.
